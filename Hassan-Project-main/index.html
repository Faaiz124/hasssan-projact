ALIGARH INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

Education enhances economic growth and human development to alleviate poverty. It frees the human spirit from the chains of ignorance and no country has succeeded without educating its people. The Aligarh Institute of Technology (AIT), Karachi was established in 1989. It is a Polytechnic Institute for young women and men that provides theoretical and practical courses of instructions in a number of technologies leading to Diploma of Associate Engineer (DAE) awarded by the Sindh Board of Technical Education. AIT has been making consistent efforts to assess the future needs of Technical Education in Pakistan
